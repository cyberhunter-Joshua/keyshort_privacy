Keyshort Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 08, 2026

Keyshort (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is an Android app supported by advertising. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you use Keyshort, how it is used, and your choices.

Information We Collect

  • Advertising data (AdMob): The app uses Google AdMob to show ads. AdMob may collect information such as device identifiers, IP address, approximate location (derived from IP), and ad interaction data for ad delivery, measurement, fraud prevention, and (where applicable) ad personalization.
  • Data you provide: Keyshort does not require you to create an account. If you contact us by email, we will receive the information you send (e.g., your email address and message).
  • App diagnostics (optional): If your device or Google Play services provides crash reports, they may include basic diagnostic information to help improve stability.

How We Use Information

  • To display and measure ads (via AdMob).
  • To keep the app secure and prevent fraud/abuse (including ad fraud detection).
  • To respond to support requests you send to us.
  • To improve reliability and fix crashes.

Third-Party Services (AdMob)

Keyshort uses Google AdMob. Google may use collected data according to its own policies. You can read Google’s Privacy Policy here: https://policies.google.com/privacy .

Ad personalization: Depending on your region and settings, you may see personalized or non-personalized ads. You can manage ad personalization in your Google account settings and on your device.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only:

  • With AdMob/Google to serve and measure ads, and to prevent fraud (as described above).
  • If required by law, legal process, or to protect rights and safety.

Data Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your data from unauthorized access.

Children’s Privacy

Keyshort is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child provided personal information, contact us so we can delete it.

Your Choices

  • Ad controls: You can manage ad personalization in your Google settings and your device settings (where available).
  • Uninstall: You can stop all collection by uninstalling the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version on this page and update the “Last updated” date.
